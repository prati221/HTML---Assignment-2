Masala Maggi Recipe

Ingredients:

• 1 packet of Maggi noodles
• ½ cup water
• ¼ cup chopped onions
• 1 teaspoon butter
• 1 Maggi tastemaker sachet

Steps:

1. Heat butter in a pan.
2. Add chopped onions and cook for 1 minute.
3. Add ½ cup water and bring it to a boil.
4. Add the noodles and tastemaker.
5. Cook at about 100°C for 2–3 minutes.
6. Serve hot.

Important Note: Do not overcook the maggi noodles.

Tip: Add cheese or sweet corn for extra taste.

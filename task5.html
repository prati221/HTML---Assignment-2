Science Facts: The Solar System

The Solar System consists of the Sun and the objects that revolve around it, including planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.

Light travels at approximately 3 × 108 meters per second.

Water has the chemical formula H2O and carbon dioxide has the formula CO2.

In mathematics: 12 ÷ 3 = 4, 5 ≠ 7, 3 ≤ 5, and 10 ≥ 8.
